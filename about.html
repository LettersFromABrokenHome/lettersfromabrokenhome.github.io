Hello! I am Donald Carmichael. Well that’s a pseudonym. But formalities aside, I am an inspirational speech writer. No, it’s not a job and my speeches are unlike what you’ve heard in high-school or in your day to day. Just telling people they can isn’t enough. You need to tell them why they can.


I have been writing these speeches since I was in the eighth grade, for my friends when something went awry for them. My library of speeches grew quickly, until I was encouraged to culminate them in a publicly accessible library.


The name of the site is named after a song from one of my favorite bands. The band in question, Green Day released a song in their album American Idiot, called Jesus of Suburbia. This song had a segment in it dubbed “Tales from Another Broken Home”. I, of course, changed “Tales” to “Letters” since it’s a better fitted name for what you may find on this site.


My  speeches are 100% AI free. That’s my promise, I hate AI as much as I hate the Devil himself. I do feel the need to include this disclaimer because I am asked that, a lot. 




As a sort of legal disclaimer, I feel I need to disclose I am not a therapist and my writings should not substitute actual therapy. If you are having serious thoughts of self harm please seek professional help.
